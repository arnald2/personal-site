Unprofessional Resume

I mainly wanted to make a little timeline widget, and then felt obliglated to fill it in with something, so here is as much of my life story as i think it's reasonable to force upon a random stranger. Enjoy!

1990

Cuba

90s Cuban life was not great for most adults, but not terrible for kids. At least not for a kid that only wanted to eat rice and fried eggs every meal.
2001

Moving to the 305

Landed in the U.S. thinking everyone lived in the Home Alone mansion. That was not quite the case, but with the shock of getting access to video games, blockbuster video, air conditioning and hot showers, it took me 10 years to notice.
2013

Adulting

Landed my first full time job with Booze Allen Hamilton in the D.C area. Really hated the cold weather, but once spring came really started enjoying all the free museums and walkability of the city.
2017

California Dreaming

Moved to California, land of granola and 8 hour hikes to start a new job with Amazon.
2018

I Do

Married a Jersey girl. Honeymooned in Moorea, where I faced off against the natural predator of Cubans (sharks)... luckily for me, they were little.
2019

Jersey Life

Moved back to the East Coast, to the burgeoning metropolis that is Jersey City. It was pretty great till the pandemic hit. Then it was just so so.
2021

New Dad

Became a father. The hospital letting us take the baby home with zero supervision, after 48 hours of not sleeping felt like a massive oversight on their part at the time. But it all worked out, baby is still around.
2024

Round Two

Welcomed baby girl #2. Twice the chaos and half the sleep, but can't imagine life without those two rowdy girls.
2026

Right Now

The present day. Trying to enjoy the little moments.
